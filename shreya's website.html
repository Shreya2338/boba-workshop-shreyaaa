Symptoms: Sometimes, our bodies send us signals when something isn't quite right. These signals can be things like coughing, sneezing, or feeling tired. When doctors try to figure out what's wrong, they ask about these signs and symptoms!

Diagnosis: Doctors use special tools and tests, just like detectives, to find out what's making you feel unwell. They might use stethoscopes, thermometers, or even X-rays to help them discover the mystery!

Health Care Trivia

1.What is the largest human organ?

The skin is the largest human organ.

2.What is the primary function of the red blood cells?

The primary function of red blood cells is to transport oxygen throughout the body.

3.What is the chemical symbol for table salt?

The chemical symbol for table salt is NaCl (Sodium Chloride).

4.What is the powerhouse of the cell?

The powerhouse of the cell is the mitochondrion (mitochondria plural).

5.What do the letters CPR stand for in first aid?

CPR stands for Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation.
